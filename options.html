To update a new password:

1) Enter your old password, if none, leave it blank.

2) Enter your new password in both "New Password" and "Confirm Password".

3) If successful, a text "Password set!" will be shown below.

To reset your old password:

1) Enter your old password, if none, leave it blank.

2) Leave both "New Password" and "Confirm Password" blank too.

3) If successful, a text "Password reset!" will be shown below.
